Model Assumptions

Current calculations are approximate and intended for comparison, not safety prediction.

  • Torque current, power load, thermal load, and duty cycle are simplified rails.
  • Battery current and phase current are now separated with approximate conversion.
  • Duty cycle approximates voltage headroom from speed, tire size, Kv, and pack voltage.
  • Motor temperature uses estimated copper/iron/overload losses, stator thermal mass, and speed-based cooling.
  • Nose scrape and tail slide only start after footpad contact geometry reaches the ground.
